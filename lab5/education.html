Education History

Kindergarten

Commercial Kindergarten

The school name is Commercial Kindergarten. And my favorite class is the art class. Teacher would give us lots of time to paint by ourselves. I was honored the first prize in the writting competition.

Primary School

DongMen Primary School

The school name is DongMen priamry school. My favorite class in primary school is music class. Students all would sing together or a single one was selected to present. I was elected to be the captain of the school basketball team and led our team to be the 2nd place in the whole provience.

Junior High School

Experimental Junior High School

The name is Experimental Junior High School. My favorite class is math class, just because I was good at math. In junior high school, we usually had lots of time after, so wo wolud like to play cards together. Since then, my friends and I are interested in playing cards and mahjong.

Senior High School

The name is Deron High school. There is no favorite class since the high school life is so boring, and we had to study all the day from morning till night. Maybe physics class is some kind of interesting than the others beacause the teacher would like to talk a lot of his funny experience.
